About Us

Perpetual Sadaqa Fund is dedicated to fostering long-term charitable impact through sustainable giving, inspired by the principles of Islam. Our mission is to uplift communities by funding projects that provide continuous benefits, ensuring that every donation has a lasting effect.

Our Values

We are guided by core Islamic values, ensuring that every contribution is used in ways that align with faith-based ethics and community welfare:

  • Ikhlas (Sincerity): All efforts are carried out with pure intentions, seeking only the pleasure of Allah (SWT).
  • Barakah (Blessings): Donations are directed toward projects that offer long-term benefits and bring prosperity to those in need.
  • Amanah (Trust): We uphold transparency and accountability, ensuring that every contribution is managed with the utmost integrity.
  • Justice & Compassion: We prioritize supporting vulnerable communities with fairness and kindness, following the principles of equity in Islam.

The Islamic Tradition of Sadaqah Jariyah

In Islam, **Sadaqah Jariyah** (continuous charity) is a noble form of giving that continues to benefit people even after the donor has passed away. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said:

“When a person dies, all their deeds come to an end except three: **Sadaqah Jariyah (continuous charity), beneficial knowledge, and a righteous child who prays for them.**” — (Sahih Muslim)

Through the Perpetual Sadaqa Fund, we strive to establish initiatives that generate ongoing rewards for donors, such as clean water projects, education, and community development.

Why Your Support Matters

By contributing to our fund, you are not only providing immediate relief but also creating a legacy of positive change. Every well built, every meal provided, and every educational program supported is a source of ongoing blessings.
