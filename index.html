Welcome to Perpetual Sadaqa Fund

Islamic Donations: A Path to Everlasting Rewards

In Islam, giving charity (sadaqah) is a fundamental act of worship that brings immense rewards. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "When a person dies, his deeds come to an end except for three: Sadaqah Jariyah (continuous charity), knowledge which benefits others, or a righteous child who prays for him." (Muslim)

Types of Islamic Charity

  • Zakat: A mandatory donation (2.5% of wealth) given to those in need.
  • Sadaqah: Voluntary charity given at any time to help others.
  • Sadaqah Jariyah: A continuous charity that benefits people long-term, such as building a mosque, school, or well.
  • Waqf: A form of perpetual charity where an asset is donated for ongoing community benefits.

Why Donate?

Your contributions help fund essential projects like clean water access, education, healthcare, and supporting orphans. Every donation is an opportunity to earn ongoing rewards in this life and the hereafter.
