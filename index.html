About Us

Perpetual Sadaqa Fund is dedicated to fostering long-term charitable impact through sustainable giving, inspired by the principles of Islam.

Our Values

  • Ikhlas (Sincerity): All efforts are carried out with pure intentions, seeking only the pleasure of Allah (SWT).
  • Barakah (Blessings): Donations are directed toward projects that offer long-term benefits.
  • Amanah (Trust): We uphold transparency and accountability.
  • Justice & Compassion: Supporting communities with fairness and kindness.

Our Projects

We support initiatives including education, clean water, and healthcare programs, ensuring donations create a lasting impact.
